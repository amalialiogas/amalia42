Hi, I'm Amalia.

I'm the Director of IT at The Study, an independent school in Montreal where I spend my days building things — infrastructure, educational technology, and curriculum for students from kindergarten to Grade 11.

This site is where I put everything that doesn't fit inside a work doc. Experiments in vibe coding, photos I'm proud of, tutorials I wish had existed when I was learning, and notes on what I'm thinking about.

My first website was created in the early 1990s using HTML (I know. I'm dating myself). Since then, I have been using HTML, CSS, Python amongst many others.

The 42 in the URL is, of course, the answer. I'm still working on the question.
