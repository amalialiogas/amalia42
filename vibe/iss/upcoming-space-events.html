Spacecraft servicing

Swift Boost

Date: No earlier than June 2026

Category: Science mission extension

Status: NASA launch schedule

Katalyst Space Technologies will attempt to rendezvous with NASA's Neil Gehrels Swift Observatory and raise its orbit.

The mission is meant to extend Swift's science life while demonstrating future spacecraft servicing capability.
